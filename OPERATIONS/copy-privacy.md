# Privacy Policy

## Component: LastUpdated
*Last Updated: January 2025*

## Component: Introduction

Hamsa Wolf ("we," "our," or "the Company") is a boutique Sales Engineering and Systems Architecture agency that provides custom-built business tools, automation systems, and AI-powered workflows. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Component: InfoCollected

We may collect the following categories of information:

### A. Information You Provide Directly

- Name, email address, phone number
- Business name and role
- Project requirements
- Intake form responses
- Files or documents you upload

### B. Automatically Collected Information

- Browser type and operating system
- IP address
- Cookies and usage analytics
- Pages visited on our site

### C. Project & System Data

For clients engaging in system builds or architecture:

- Business workflow information
- Operational processes
- Data models, automation logic, and internal structures
- Any content required to design custom modules or tools

### D. Communication Records

- Emails
- Scheduling information
- Meeting recordings (when explicitly permitted)

**We do not sell personal data.**

### Component: InfoUse

We use collected information to:
